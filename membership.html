Questions

FAQ

Can I try before I buy?+
Yes! Every new member gets their first week completely free — no credit card required. Just create an account, pick a plan, and start training.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Absolutely. Monthly memberships can be cancelled at any time with no fees. Annual memberships include a 30-day cancellation window from the start date.
How many locations can I access?+
Starter members get access to 1 home location. Pro and Elite members get access to all East Blue studio locations worldwide.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?+
Yes, you can change your plan at any time through your account dashboard. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a family or corporate plan?+
Yes! We offer discounted family bundles and corporate wellness plans for teams of 5 or more. Contact us directly for custom pricing.
