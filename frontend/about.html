Our Story

ABOUT EAST BLUE

We believe fitness is for everyone. Our mission is to make life-changing training accessible, inclusive, and genuinely fun.

Who We Are

BORN FROM A
PASSION

East Blue was founded in 2018 by a group of athletes, coaches, and community builders who believed that the best workouts happen together. Tired of impersonal gym experiences, we set out to create something different — a place where every member is known by name, every session is crafted with purpose, and every result is celebrated.

What started as a single studio has grown into a global network of passionate fitness communities. Our science-backed methods, world-class coaching, and unwavering community spirit have made East Blue the fastest-growing group fitness brand in the world.

What We Stand For

OUR VALUES

01

Community First

Every decision we make starts with our members. We build environments where people support, push, and celebrate each other — not just compete.

02

Science-Backed

Our programming is rooted in exercise science, physiology, and decades of coaching experience. We don't follow trends — we set them.

03

For Every Body

Fitness is not one-size-fits-all. Our classes are designed to be scaled and modified for every fitness level, age, and background.

04

Radical Transparency

We're honest about our pricing, our methods, and our results. No gimmicks, no hidden fees, no miracle promises.

05

Continuous Innovation

We invest heavily in R&D to ensure every class, every challenge, and every tool we provide is best-in-class. We never stop improving.

06

Global Impact

From local communities to global challenges, we believe that healthier people build healthier societies. Our mission is bigger than just fitness.
