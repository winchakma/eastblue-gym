The Programme

HOW IT WORKS

Eight focused weeks of progressive training and nutrition, designed to transform your body and mindset.

Week 1–2
Foundation Phase
Establish your baseline. Learn proper form, technique and movement patterns. Nutrition kickstart with our dietitian team.
Week 3–4
Build Phase
Increase load and intensity. Introduce progressive overload principles. Mid-challenge measurement and nutrition recalibration.
Week 5–6
Push Phase
Maximum intensity sessions. Cardio and strength combined. Community accountability check-ins every 3 days.
Week 7–8
Peak & Transform
Peak performance workouts. Final measurements. Before/after submissions. Prize ceremony and celebration event.
